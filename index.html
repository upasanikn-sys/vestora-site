How this is calculated
  • Fat-tailed Monte Carlo simulation over your inputs (corpus, SIP, expenses, horizon).
  • Return and inflation assumptions are editable in the app; the score shown assumes a flat SIP with no step-up.
  • The score is the share of simulated paths that sustain your target — an illustration, not a prediction.
  • Limitations: no tax drag modelling per path, no sequence-of-returns guarantees, sample data shown here.
