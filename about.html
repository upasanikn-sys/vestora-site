About Vestora

An independent, India-based software project · in Windows early access
The short version: Vestora exists because financial apps kept asking for the one thing they should never need — a copy of your financial life on someone else's server. Vestora is built the other way around: one private application, on your own device, honest about what it can and cannot do.

Why Vestora was built

Personal finance in India is scattered — bank apps, broker apps, spreadsheets, tax portals, insurance PDFs, and a dozen cloud services each holding a fragment of your life. The tools that promise to unify it usually do so by uploading everything to their cloud, monetising the data, or charging forever for access to your own numbers.

Vestora is a deliberate rejection of that trade. It unifies your financial picture — net worth, cash flow, investments, goals, debt, retirement, documents — in software that runs on your device, keeps its database with you, and earns money the old-fashioned way: by selling software, not data.

The principles it is built on

  • Local-first, honestly. Your database stays on your device; what is and isn't encrypted is documented precisely in the security architecture — including current limitations.
  • Never fake a number. If a price feed is down, Vestora says "unavailable". Projections show their assumptions. Sample data is labelled as sample data.
  • The Free plan is real. Track, edit and export everything, forever. Paid tiers add depth; they never hold your data hostage.
  • Honest marketing. No invented users, no fake reviews, no security theatre. What you read on this site is checked against the shipped product.

Who is behind it

Vestora is designed, built and supported by an independent developer in India. Support email replies come from the person who wrote the code.

A fuller founder profile and the registered operating entity will be published here before paid launch.

Where the product stands today

  • Windows early access — build v2.0.0 (17 July 2026), distributed by email on request.
  • 30 redesigned screens across the desktop dashboard and phone experience.
  • Encrypted document vault and encrypted backups (AES-256-GCM), offline licensing, and a documented security architecture.
  • macOS, Android and iOS are in development; database encryption at rest is scheduled on the roadmap.
  • Release notes currently ship by email; a public changelog page is planned.

Accountability

Questions, bugs, security reports and refunds all reach a human at hello@vestora.app — normally within 48 hours. The Terms, Privacy Policy, Refund Policy and License Terms say the rest, in plain language.
