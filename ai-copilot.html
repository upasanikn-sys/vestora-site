How Vestora AI actually works

Illustrative planning output. Not investment, tax, legal or lending advice.

Deterministic at the core

Scores, projections and tax numbers come from documented financial mathematics — the same inputs always produce the same outputs. Nothing is a black box.

Rule-based insights, on your device

Recommendations are generated by transparent rules over your own data, computed locally. There is no separate AI model download and no extra hardware requirement.

Hosted AI is optional and off by default

If a hosted AI feature is offered, it is disabled until you explicitly turn it on, and what it sends is disclosed at that moment — never before.

Limits, honestly

Outputs can be wrong or incomplete, assumptions drift, and high-stakes decisions deserve a qualified professional. Vestora shows its assumptions so you can challenge them.
