Before you email — quick answers

  • Where is my data? In %USERPROFILE%\FinOSData on your own device. Back up that folder and you have everything.
  • The app won't start? SmartScreen may warn on the early-access build ("More info" → "Run anyway"). Verify the download's SHA-256 with us if in doubt.
  • Trial ended? Nothing is lost — the Free plan continues forever; premium features unlock with a licence key.
  • Refund? 30 days, no questions — see the refund policy.
