Frequently asked questions

Do you store my data on any servers?+

No. Vestora is 100% local-first — your data lives only on your device in an encrypted SQLite database. There is no Vestora server, no account and no cloud upload unless you explicitly choose to back up to your own cloud.

Can I use Vestora completely offline?+

Yes. Everything works fully offline. An internet connection is only used — optionally — to refresh live market prices from free public feeds.

Will my data be safe if I change my device?+

Yes. You can export an encrypted backup and restore it on your new device, or point Vestora at your own encrypted cloud folder. You always hold the keys — Vestora never does.

Can I cancel or switch plans anytime?+

Absolutely. Cancel or change plans whenever you like. If you cancel a paid plan you simply drop to the Free plan — still fully usable forever, with all your data readable and exportable.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?+

Yes — you get a full Pro trial to explore every premium feature. After it ends you continue on the generous Free plan for as long as you like, and can upgrade anytime.

How do paid plans work without an account?+

Your financial data never needs a login — it lives only on your device. When you buy a plan, we email you a signed license key; enter it once and it activates offline, forever. The only thing we ever hold is the purchase email used to send your key. Changing devices? Just re-enter your key.

Does Vestora connect to my bank?+

No — by design. You import CSV statements (one-click presets for HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Axis and Kotak, plus auto-detect) or add transactions yourself. No credentials are ever stored, so nothing can leak. Automatic bank connections are not supported.

What is the refund policy?+

30 days, no questions asked, on paid plans. Email hello@vestora.app with your order id and the refund goes back to your original payment method. Your data stays on your device either way — the app simply returns to the Free plan.
